ABOUT

Reza is graduated from K.N.Toosi University of Technology in Iran with a Master's degree in Geographic Information Science (GIS). During his master studies, he worked on developing a multi-objective optimization model for urban land-use allocation in Tehran, Iran, using multi-objective meta-heuristic algorithms and spatial metrics, considering the four objectives of : (1) Compatibility between adjacent land uses, (2) Land suitability for each land-uses class, (3) Accessibility between land-uses, and (4) Spatial growth pattern of the city.

He has been working in FANAP ICT as a GIS specialist and spatial software developer for the last 2 years, working on design and implementation of GIS web services for "Pasargad Bank" in order to provide a real-time path planing service for replenishment vehicles and optimizing AMTs' network by the use of meta-heuristic algorithms. He has enough proficiency on programming languages including Python, JavaScript, Java and MATLAB along with good experience on handling Databases.

He earned rank 30th among 3000 applicants in the national master entrance exam, Iran. Additionally, he has a demonstrated background in teamwork and team management as he was elected as a member of geomatics academic committee of Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST) throughout his Bachelor. He is always passionate to develop GIS services and software, provide GIS solutions, and to work on challenging and real world spatial problems.

If you want to know more about the projects he has done, check his Github, and see his Vitae.
